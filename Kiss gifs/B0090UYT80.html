    4.0 out of 5 stars A Challenging Variation Of The Rubik's Cube
    Reviewed in the United States on June 6, 2016
    Size: Value not foundColor: Black2#Verified Purchase
    This is a well-made variation of a Rubik's Cube. It is, however, much more difficult to solve, and I've yet to solve this one myself.

    There are two additional challenges with this cube: 1) all the surfaces are the same silver color, which means 2) you have to be familiar with the shapes on all the sides when you get this, as no sides are the sameeven though they look similar; the shapes of the sides are your color reference.

    The silver parts are made of quality stickers that look like they will withstand wear and tear. I took a star off because the movement of the cube could be smoother. I have a lot of Rubik's Cubes in my home so I have an idea of what makes a "good" cube. If anything, the motion from one movement to another is good enough for most cubes, as this one is.

    This can be a fun Rubik's Cube if you can solve it. Recommended.
